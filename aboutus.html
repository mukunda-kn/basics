About Us

Poly Valve Tech the premier manufacturer of Industrial Valves established in August 2012. We also engaged in supply of spares,maintenance and reconditioning of all types of valves.

Promoter of this company has worked in a Reputed valve manufacturing company, at various capacities in Production,R&D & Quality assurance, Safety, Administration dept. Our organization is located at Gokul Road Industrial Estate, Hubballi,an industrially developed city in Karnataka which is well connected by road, rail & air.

The organization has vision to supply quality products. The organization has mission to serve the industry with quality products domestically and also overseas clients with wide range of products used in petrochemicals, fertilizers, food and beverages,Pharmaceuticals, power sectors and steel industries etc.

The management has rich experience in developing valves with sound knowledge of engineering with well equipped design and manufacturing facilities. Our quality products have a high standard of design & accuracy that meet domestic as well as international specifications.

POLY VALVE TECH is growing as a premium quality valve manufacturer in India.
